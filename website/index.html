Where the music comes from

We aggregate music from the world's best free and legal sources. Every track is Creative Commons licensed or public domain.

600,000+
Jamendo
The world's largest Creative Commons music catalog. Indie artists across every genre — electronic, rock, jazz, hip-hop, ambient, classical, and more.
30,000+
Radio Browser
A community-maintained database of internet radio stations from around the world. Live streams, 24/7, every genre and language.
Millions
Internet Archive
The digital library of the internet. Live concert recordings, vintage albums, spoken word, and public domain audio spanning decades.

Music belongs to everyone

We believe access to music is a fundamental part of being human. It shouldn't require a credit card, an account, or sitting through advertisements.

Hundreds of thousands of artists have chosen to share their work freely under Creative Commons licenses. They want to be heard. We're building the stage.

  • ✓
    Open source. Our code is on GitHub. Anyone can inspect it, contribute to it, or fork it.
  • ✓
    Artist-first. We link back to artists. We respect their licenses. We help them get discovered.
  • ✓
    No venture capital. No investors pushing us to monetize your attention. This is a community project.
  • ✓
    Privacy by design. No accounts, no analytics, no data collection. We literally can't track you.
  • ✓
    Forever free. Not freemium. Not "free with ads." Not "free trial." Free. Period.
