Johann Sebastian Bach

1685–1750

German composer and musician of the Baroque period. Known for the Brandenburg Concertos, Mass in B minor, The Well-Tempered Clavier, and numerous cantatas, chorales, and organ works. Widely regarded as one of the greatest composers in Western musical history.
