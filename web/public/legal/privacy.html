Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Summary

FreeMusic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No account or login data
  • No listening history sent to any server
  • No analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • No device identifiers or IP address logging
  • No advertising data

Local Storage

FreeMusic stores your preferences (favorites, playlists, search history, blacklist) locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

FreeMusic connects to the following third-party APIs to stream music and radio:

  • Jamendo API (api.jamendo.com) - Creative Commons music catalog. Subject to Jamendo's Privacy Policy.
  • Radio Browser API (radio-browser.info) - Community radio station database. Open source, no tracking.
  • Internet Archive (archive.org) - Public domain audio. Subject to Archive.org's Terms.

When you play music, your device connects directly to these services. We do not proxy or intercept these connections.

Children's Privacy

FreeMusic does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page. Since we collect nothing, changes are unlikely.
